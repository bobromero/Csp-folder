Sea Turtle Light Watch!

Baby Sea Turtles are attracted to light after hatching from their egg.

They Normally go towards the moon light to find their way to the ocean.

The Problem

Light polution near beaches that are inhabited by baby sea turtles causes them to go towards the unnatural light into the street rather than the moonlight going into the ocean.

Sea Turtles are also affected by pollution in the ocean. Straws,soda can wrapping, plastic bags, and chemicals like fuel.

Locations

There are numerous locations across the state of Florida. Each of these locations offer service to turtles that are in need.

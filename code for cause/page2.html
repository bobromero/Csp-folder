Mission Statement

We here at Sea Turtle Light Watch are committed to working for as long as we can to stop the problem that baby Sea Turtles face. We will ensure that all lights near the beach will be turned off at night, and that any Sea Turtle nest will be reported to proper authority. We pledge to make the beaches safe for baby turtles, so that our great grandkids will be able to see them like we do.
